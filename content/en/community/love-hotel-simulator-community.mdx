<Section icon={Shield} title="Staff Management and Operational Efficiency">

As your hotel grows, manual management becomes impossible. Hiring the right staff is a milestone frequently discussed within the **love hotel simulator community**. Efficient employees can handle cleaning, security, and front-desk duties, allowing you to focus on the broader business strategy. However, staff management introduces its own set of challenges, including salary costs and performance monitoring.

Training your staff is just as important as hiring them. A well-trained maid will clean rooms 30% faster, reducing the "downtime" between guests. Similarly, a skilled receptionist can handle difficult customers, preventing potential reputation damage.
