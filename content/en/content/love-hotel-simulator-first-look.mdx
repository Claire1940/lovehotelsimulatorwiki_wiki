A hotel cannot run on a single owner's labor forever. The transition from a renovation project to a functioning business involves hiring staff and exploring the neighborhood. In **love hotel simulator**, the recruitment process is active; you must walk around the neighborhood and convince potential employees to join your venture.

Staffing is the backbone of the late-game experience. You need individuals who can handle the front desk, manage room service, and maintain the cleanliness you worked so hard to establish.
