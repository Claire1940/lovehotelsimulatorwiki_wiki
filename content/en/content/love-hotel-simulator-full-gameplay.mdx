<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Renovation and Cleaning Phase">

The core loop of the early **love hotel simulator full gameplay** revolves around sanitation. You cannot place furniture in a room that is currently a "factory of stench." Players must manually interact with trash piles and debris to clear space. This phase is tedious but rewarding, as it reveals the true potential of the floor plan. The game emphasizes the contrast between the exterior barn-like appearance and the interior potential, challenging players to look past the surface grime.

Cleaning is not just about aesthetics; it directly impacts the "Room Appeal" stat, which dictates how much you can charge per hour. A room with lingering trash will result in immediate customer complaints and zero revenue. Therefore, thoroughness is rewarded during this initial renovation stage.
