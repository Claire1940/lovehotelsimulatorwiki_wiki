### The Role of the Stalker
The Stalker is a unique late-game unit unlocked at 4 stars. Unlike other staff, the Stalker's primary job is automation. They wander the hotel and refill Vending Machines and Condom Dispensers automatically. Once you have a few Stalkers, you no longer need to manually click on machines to restock them, allowing you to focus on expansion.

<Section icon={Target} title="Attracting Special Guests and VIPs">

As your hotel grows, you will attract non-human entities with specific needs and high spending power. Understanding these guests is key to reaching the 6-star maximum rating.

**Sky Fairs:**
These influential beings arrive via a secondary entrance on higher floors. They exclusively use **Missionary** and **Balloon** suites. Showing them a good time is the fastest way to boost your reputation during the mid-game.

**Astronauts:**
Arriving with "Space Cash," these guests are extremely wealthy. They only stay in **Tropical** and **Banana** suites. Because they pay significantly more than regular humans, you should prioritize building these rooms once you hit 5 stars.

**Devil Spawn:**
These horny creatures emerge from the ground if you dig deep enough. They prefer **Chocolate** and **Heaven** suites. Interestingly, they never bring their own condoms, so having a well-stocked dispenser nearby is mandatory for their satisfaction.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Financial Optimization and End-Game Goals">

To reach the ultimate goal of being the best love hotel in the world, you must manage your finances carefully. The biggest drain on your resources will be the electricity bill and staff salaries.

**Managing the Electricity Bill:**
Every room and machine you build adds to your monthly bill. If your income doesn't keep up, you'll go bankrupt. Use the "Fast Forward" feature sparingly until you have enough rooms to ensure a constant flow of cash.

**Kitchen and Dining Optimization:**
At 3 stars, you unlock the Kitchen and Dining Room.
- **Cooks**: Required to run the kitchen.
- **Freezers**: Unlocked at 4 stars; these speed up cooking time significantly.
- **Dining Rooms**: Guests will visit these when they are hungry, providing an extra source of income outside of room rentals.
