<Section icon={Map} title="Building Strategy and Vertical Layout Optimization">

Managing your structural expansion is the foundation of a highly profitable establishment. In this game, players can expand their hotel layout both upward by building new floors and downward by excavation. Balancing your vertical growth is essential because a taller building increases the travel time for your staff, particularly Bellhops and Cleaners. 

When planning your floor layouts, group similar room types together to streamline service delivery. Lower floors should ideally house high-turnover, low-cost rooms like Missionary and Spooning suites, while higher floors should be reserved for luxury themed suites. This setup ensures that standard guests exit quickly, keeping the lobby clear for high-paying VIPs.

### Underground Excavation and Hidden Treasures

Do not ignore the space beneath your lobby. Digging underground is not only an excellent way to place utility rooms out of sight, but it also triggers lucrative random events. During excavation, your construction crew has a high chance of uncovering buried treasure chests. Even after paying your crew their cut, these chests can instantly inject up to $20,000 into your treasury, providing the necessary capital to fund expensive luxury suites on upper floors.
