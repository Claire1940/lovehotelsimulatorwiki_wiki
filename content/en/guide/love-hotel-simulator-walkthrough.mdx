<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Furniture Procurement and Layout Strategy">

Furnishing your rooms is where you start to define the style and class of your establishment. In the beginning, your budget is tight, meaning you must choose your purchases wisely. You do not need the most expensive items immediately; instead, focus on fulfilling the basic requirements of a functional room. A standard bed and basic lighting are the bare minimum needed to open a room for business.

When arranging furniture, spatial awareness is key. Ensure there is a clear path from the door to the bed so guests can navigate the room without getting stuck. Placing items too close together can cause pathfinding issues for the AI, which may lead to negative reviews.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Placement Tip">
Place your bed near the primary power outlets. This allows you to add electronic amenities, such as sound systems or specialized lighting, later without reorganizing the entire room layout.
</Callout>
