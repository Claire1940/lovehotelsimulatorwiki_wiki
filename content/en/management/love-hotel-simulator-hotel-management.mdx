As your hotel expands, managing every room manually becomes impossible. You will need to hire staff to handle cleaning, reception duties, and security. Recruiting the right personnel reduces your operational workload and ensures that rooms are turned over quickly for the next set of customers.

  <Step num="1" title="Scavenge and Clean">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Furnish and Theme">

  <Step num="3" title="Hire Operational Staff">
