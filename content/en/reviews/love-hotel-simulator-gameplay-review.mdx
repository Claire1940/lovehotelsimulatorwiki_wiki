<Section icon={Trophy} title="Progression Guide: From Small Motel to Luxury Hotel">

Progressing in Love Hotel Simulator requires a balance of aggressive expansion and careful resource management. Following a structured growth path ensures you don't go bankrupt during the early, vulnerable stages of your business.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Foundation Phase">
    Start by building two basic rooms. Focus on functionality over form. Use the initial capital to hire one part-time janitor so you can focus on guest check-ins and marketing.

  <Step num="2" title="Reputation Building">

  <Step num="3" title="Expansion and Automation">
