<Section icon={Shield} title="Staff Management and Operational Efficiency">

As your hotel grows, you can no longer do everything yourself. This is where the staff management system comes into play. Hiring the right team is crucial for scaling your business from a three-room operation to a massive complex.

You can hire staff for various roles:
- **Receptionists**: Handle check-ins and guest inquiries.
- **Cleaning Crew**: Ensure rooms are sanitized and reset for the next guest.
- **Maintenance Techs**: Repair broken furniture and electrical systems.
- **Security**: Prevent disturbances and handle unruly guests.

Managing shifts is also a part of the strategy. Night shifts are typically busier and require more cleaning staff, while day shifts might focus more on maintenance and upgrades. Balancing your payroll against your daily income is the ultimate test of your management skills.
