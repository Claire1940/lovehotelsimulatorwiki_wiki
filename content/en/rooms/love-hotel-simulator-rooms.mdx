| Cleaning Phase | Primary Task | Tools Required | Impact on Room |

| **De-cluttering** | Remove large trash items | Manual Labor | Increases walkable space |

| **Structural Fix** | Repairing holes and leaks | Repair Tools | Permits furniture placement |
| **Final Polish** | Dusting and window cleaning | Microfiber Cloth | Boosts initial client rating |

The transition from a "wreck" to a "habitable space" is the most labor-intensive part of the early game. You must physically interact with the grime, ensuring that every corner of the room meets the minimum standards for a love hotel.

A room without a name is just a box. To truly master **love hotel simulator rooms**, you must give each space an identity. Naming rooms helps in managing your ledger and creates a "story" for your hotel that attracts more clients.
